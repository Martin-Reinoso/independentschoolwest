Our Foundation

Why Choose Families for Education?

We believe the best education happens when school and home work as one. Our three pillars guide everything we do.

✝

Faith & Character

Rooted in Catholic tradition, we form young people in virtue, purpose, and a deep relationship with God — preparing them for life, not just exams.

📚

Academic Excellence

Rigorous, engaging education with high expectations. Every student is known by name and guided to reach their full potential.

🤝

Parent Partnership

Parents are the first educators. Our school is an extension of the home — a true partnership where families and teachers walk the journey together.

Our Story

Inspired by a Proven Model

Inspired by the global Pared model — a network of parent-partnered schools operating successfully across multiple continents — we are bringing this vision to Melbourne's west.

The Pared model has produced outstanding outcomes in faith, character, and academics because it treats the family as the heart of education. Parents are not passive observers — they are active co-educators.

Melbourne's west is a growing, vibrant community full of families seeking exactly this kind of school. Our goal is to open our doors in 2027 and serve those families for generations.
