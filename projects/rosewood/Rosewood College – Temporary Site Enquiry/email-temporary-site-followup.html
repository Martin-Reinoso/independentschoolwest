Dear [Principal / Business Manager],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. I appreciate your willingness to consider our enquiry, and I hope our conversation gave you a useful overview of who we are and what we are trying to achieve.

As discussed, Families for Education is a non-profit organisation in the process of registering Rosewood College — an independent co-ed Catholic primary school (Prep – Grade 5) planned to open in Melton in 2027, subject to VRQA approval. As part of the registration process, the VRQA has recommended we secure a temporary site arrangement with an existing school while our permanent site planning permit is being finalised.

We are looking to accommodate approximately 20–30 students in 1–2 classrooms for a period of up to 12 months. We are flexible in how this could work — whether through the use of existing spare classrooms, a separate area of your site, or even portable classrooms that we would install and remove at our own cost. Our aim is to operate as a self-contained program with minimal disruption to your school community.

I have attached a short one-pager with more detail about Rosewood College and what we are seeking. Please don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or would like to explore this further — we would welcome the opportunity to meet in person to discuss.
