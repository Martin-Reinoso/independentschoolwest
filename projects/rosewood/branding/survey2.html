The goal of this survey is to choose a path forward. Your preference will contribute to that decision. But because the selected direction will be developed further, it is just as important to provide honest feedback on both — whichever one the board selects, the designer needs your notes on it.
Q1
Which direction do you prefer?
Select one. This is your vote for the path forward.
Please select a direction before continuing.
Part A — Your preferred direction
Q2
What specifically makes your top-ranked emblem the strongest option?
Be as concrete as you can — what does it communicate, and why does that matter for Rosewood College?
Please share your reasoning.
Q3
What refinements would make your preferred direction stronger?
Think about what the designer should develop further — these notes will go directly into the brief.
Please share your thoughts on refinements.
Part B — The second option
Q4
What is the strongest aspect of the second option?
Even if it is not your preference, engage with what it does well. If it were selected, what would be worth building on?
Please share what works well in the second option.
Q5
What would need to improve for you to feel confident if the second option were selected?
Not a list of complaints — a focused brief. If the board chose this direction, what should the designer address first?
Please share what would need to improve.
Part C — School identity
Q6
Is there anything essential to Rosewood College's identity that is missing from both directions?
A value, a feeling, or a symbol that neither design captures — something that should be present in whatever direction is developed.
Part D — Ratings
Q7
Rate each direction across three criteria.
1 is weak, 5 is excellent. Be honest — this is designed to surface real differences between the two, not to give everything a 5.
