Families for Education is a group of Catholic families working to establish a new independent Catholic school in Melbourne's West. The project has been years in the making and is now moving into an exciting new stage.

This session is for Catholic parents who are hearing about the project for the first time, as well as families already following the journey.

At a glance

Printed on bulletins, shared in parishes, or passed on to a friend, this session is a simple way to learn what the school could offer families in the West and where the project is heading next.
