Thank you to all who joined our Online Information Session on Wednesday 20th May 2026. It was a significant milestone for Families for Education and a joy to share the latest progress of the project with so many families and supporters.

During the evening we publicly announced the name of our school: Rosewood College. We also shared updates on the permanent site, fees, formation, and the next steps on the road to opening in 2027.

You can revisit the announcement and slides from the night here, and please feel free to share them with others who may wish to be part of this dream.
