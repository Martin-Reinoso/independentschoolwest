Recent Event

The SALT of the Earth

More than 70 friends of the school project joined us for our latest Ideals in Teaching event at The Club, Caroline Springs. Our special guest speaker Dr. Anne-Maire Irwin introduced the SALT (Scripture and Liturgy Teaching) Approach — a distinctive method at the heart of our school's vision for forming students in faith and character.

The SALT Approach facilitates a hands-on and interactive learning experience for students as they journey through their faith and family life. Employed in several diocesan and independent schools in Australia and New Zealand, it recognises how deeply children learn when their senses, imagination and capacity for wonder are fully engaged. SALT provides beautiful and accessible materials designed to give catechists and teachers the confidence and tools they need to bring faith to life by introducing the realities of Jesus' life on earth – the places He lived, the people He met, and the Church He established.

For those who attended, we hope the introduction to SALT was inspiring and provided a sense of why our school will be unlike any other in Melbourne's West when it comes to the religious formation of students. We encourage you to visit the SALT Approach website and sign up to the SALT Learning Hub to find out more about this amazing programme.
