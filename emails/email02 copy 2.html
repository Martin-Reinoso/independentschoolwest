Our New Website

We're now online — explore ffe.org.au

We are thrilled to share that the Families for Education website is now live at ffe.org.au. Whether you're hearing about us for the first time or have been following our journey from the very beginning, the website is now your home for everything related to the school.

We've designed it to be a clear, welcoming place where families can learn about our vision, follow our progress, read our bulletins, and find out how to get involved. We invite you to take a look and share it with anyone who might share our vision for Catholic education in Melbourne's West.

In this edition, we also walk you through what you'll find on the site and remind you of our upcoming Family Picnic on Sunday 26 April — we hope to see you there.

What's on the Site

Everything you need to know,
in one place

Here's a quick guide to what you'll find when you visit ffe.org.au:

 

Our Vision

Faith & Character, Academic Excellence, and Parent Partnership — the three pillars that guide everything we do.
 

Our Story

Learn about the Pared model, the SALT Approach, the Spalding Method, and our Founding Principal Dr Anne-Marie Irwin.
 

Progress & Milestones

A live fundraising tracker and milestone timeline — from board formation and site purchase to our 2027 opening. We're 78% of the way there, with just $2M still to raise.
 

Bulletins

All four of our bulletins — from our Vision Launch in April 2025 through to December 2025 — are available to download in one place.
 

Get Involved

Pray, share, donate or loan, and connect — four ways every family can help make this school a reality. The Expression of Interest form is just one click away.

Visit the site at ffe.org.au and please share it with family and friends who may be interested in what we are building.
