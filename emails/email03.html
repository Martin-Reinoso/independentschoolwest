Looking Back

What a great picnic — thank you

Last Sunday families gathered at Frontier Park, Aintree, for our community picnic, and it was everything we had hoped it would be. It was lovely to see the kids playing together and the vision for the new school felt more alive than ever.

Thank you to every family who came along, and to all who helped make it a warm and welcoming afternoon. This is the community we are building: families who believe that parents should be true partners in their children's education, and that a school rooted in faith can shape children who flourish for life. Days like this remind us exactly why we are doing this.
