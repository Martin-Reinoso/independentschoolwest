A Major Milestone

Our school now has a name: Rosewood College

Thank you to all who joined our Families for Education Information Night on Wednesday 20 May 2026. It was a joy to share the latest progress of the project and to announce the name of our school: Rosewood College.

It was an exciting evening, and the updates shared across the night showed just how much progress has been made in every area of planning, from master planning and location to fees, formation, and the next stages of the project.

School Name Announcement

Rosewood College

We are deeply grateful to every family helping bring this vision to life.
