Why a New School?

Our vision is to establish a Catholic school in Melbourne’s west that truly supports families in their role as the first educators. Inspired by the Pared model, the school will be an extension of the home, where parents and teachers work together to form young people in faith, character, and academic excellence. At the heart of this vision is a culture of mentoring, virtue, and partnership, so that every student is known, guided, and encouraged to grow into a person of purpose and service.
