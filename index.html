Our Foundation

Why Choose Families for Education?

We believe the best education happens when school and home work as one. Our three pillars guide everything we do.

Faith & Character

Rooted in Catholic tradition, we form young people in virtue, purpose, and a deep relationship with God — preparing them for life, not just exams.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous, engaging education with high expectations. Every student is known by name and guided to reach their full potential.

Parent Partnership

Parents are the first educators. Our school is an extension of the home — a true partnership where families and teachers walk the journey together.

Our Story

Inspired by a Proven Model

Inspired by the Pared model — a parent-partnered school model used in Australia and New Zealand — we are bringing this vision to Melbourne's west.

Our distinctive SALT (Scripture and Liturgy Teaching) approach weaves faith formation into daily learning, while the Spalding Method ensures every child builds a rock-solid foundation in literacy.

Led by our Founding Principal, Dr Anne-Marie Irwin, our school will open at 178–202 Highett Road, Melton in 2027 — serving the growing families of Melbourne's west for generations to come.
