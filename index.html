School Project Update: A Strong Start to 2025

A Vision Takes Root

If you are receiving this newsletter, it is likely because you’ve expressed interest in a new independent Catholic school planned for Melbourne’s outer-West, with a target opening date of 2027. We are excited to share that 2025 has already been a year of strong progress!

Organisational Milestones

  • The company structure is in the process of being set up.
  • Initial financial modelling for the school’s operations has been largely completed.
  • We have notified the Victorian Registration and Qualifications Authority (VRQA) of our intention to establish a school.
  • We have also started the official school registration paperwork.
  • We have started the development of the website.

Our Team Is Growing

Our steering committee has expanded to twelve dedicated members with expertise in:

  • School leadership & education
  • Finance & law
  • Business & project management
  • Fundraising
  • Marriage & family formation

Their combined experience is helping shape the foundations of the school.

Parents as Primary Educators

Why Start a New School in 2027?

There are already 1375 schools in Melbourne, but our mission is different: holistic education guided by parents and rooted in faith.

Our Educational Philosophy

Inspired by Catholic teaching, focused on the formation of the whole person. Parents are the primary educators. We aim to support, not replace, that role.

Our Inspiration: The Pared Model

  • School as an extension of the home
  • High-quality academics
  • Mentoring systems
  • Support for effective parenting
  • Virtue & character curriculum
  • Faithful, positive staff
